Side salad

A fancy little side salad for your snazzy lunch.

Ingredients

  • 4 cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 4 orange bell pepper
  • 0.5 avocado
  • 3 spring onions
  • 1 tbs of olive oil
  • 1 splash of balsamic vinegar
  • A little pepper

Containers

  • 1 Tupperware tubs

Method:

Get four cherry tomatoes and cut them in half, sprinkle quarter a salt directly onto the inside of the tomatoes for flavour. Also chop up one orange bell pepper, half an avocado, and three spring onions. Add all of these ingredients to your Tupperware tub. Drizzle over one olive oil, a splash of balsamic vinegar, a cheeky bit of pepper, and another quarter salt. Stir everything up, and you are good to go.
